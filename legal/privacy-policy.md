Mundial ("we," "us," "the app") is a World Cup tournament simulator. This
Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use Mundial,
how we use it, and the choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you create a profile to use Mundial, we collect:

- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Supported team** (the national team you choose to follow)

We also automatically log **simulation results** (tournament mode, team
counts, and outcomes such as winner/runner-up/third/fourth place) generated
when you run a simulation. These logs are not directly tied to your name or
email in a way that is displayed back to you or other users — they are
stored as a separate usage log used for aggregate statistics.

Standard technical data (such as IP address and request metadata) may also
be logged automatically by our hosting provider (Vercel) as part of normal
web server operation.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

- Operate and improve the Mundial simulator experience
- Generate aggregate, non-identifying statistics about how the app is used
  (e.g., most-supported teams, most-simulated tournament formats)
- Respond to support or data-deletion requests
- Maintain the security and integrity of the service

## 3. Where Data Is Stored

Signup and simulation data is stored using Upstash Redis, accessed through
serverless functions hosted on Vercel. Access to this stored data is
restricted to the app administrator via a password-protected admin view.
We do not display your name or email to other users of the app.

## 4. Sharing, Transfer, and Possible Sale of Information

**We may share, transfer, or sell the information described in Section 1**
(including name and email) — for example, in connection with a business
transaction (such as a sale, merger, or transfer of the app or its assets),
or to third parties for purposes not otherwise limited by this policy. If
our practices around sharing or selling personal information change
materially, we will update this policy accordingly.

We do not currently use your information for third-party advertising, and
we do not sell information to data brokers for marketing purposes as of the
"Last updated" date above. If that changes, this section will be updated to
reflect it.

## 5. Third-Party Service Providers

We rely on the following infrastructure providers to operate Mundial, each
of which may process data on our behalf under their own privacy/security
terms:

- **Vercel** — application hosting and serverless functions
- **Upstash** — Redis-based data storage

## 6. Cookies and Local Storage

Mundial uses your browser's local storage (not third-party tracking
cookies) to remember your profile and in-progress tournaments on your
device. See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for details.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Mundial is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly
collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has
provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details
below so we can remove it.

## 8. Your Rights and Data Deletion Requests

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal
information at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will make
reasonable efforts to fulfill valid requests.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
