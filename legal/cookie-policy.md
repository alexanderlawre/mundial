This Cookie Policy explains how Mundial uses your browser's local storage.
Mundial does **not** use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies,
or analytics cookies.

## 1. What We Actually Use: Local Storage, Not Tracking Cookies

Instead of traditional cookies, Mundial uses your browser's `localStorage`
to remember information **only on your own device**. This data is never
transmitted to us automatically — it simply lets the app remember your
session between visits, the same way it would if it were kept in memory.

Items currently stored:

| Key | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `mundial.profile` | Your name, email, and supported team, so you don't have to re-enter them each visit |
| `mundial.activeTournament` | Your in-progress simulation, so you can resume where you left off |
| `mundial.adminDataCache` | (Admin only) A temporary local cache of admin dashboard data, used to power the "View All" detail pages |
| `mundial.theme` | Your dark/light mode preference |
| `mundial.language` | Your selected display language |

## 2. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, advertising pixels, or any other
third-party tracking or ad-serving cookies at this time. If that changes,
this policy will be updated to reflect it.

## 3. How to Clear This Data

You can clear all locally stored Mundial data at any time by clearing your
browser's site data/local storage for this site, or by using the
in-app "Reset Profile" option where available.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Material changes will
be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
